The Challenge That Drives Us,
Introducing BEST Robotics.

01

Hands-On STEM Learning

BEST Robotics immerses middle and high school students in real-world engineering by challenging them to design, build, and program robots to improve problem-solving skills.

02

Teamwork and Collaboration

Students work in teams to tackle engineering challenges, mirroring real-world projects and emphasizing the importance of communication and cooperation.

03

Comprehensive Evaluation

Teams are assessed on their robot's performance, engineering notebook, marketing presentation, and overall team spirit, promoting a well-rounded skill set.

04

Nationwide Participation

Since 1993, BEST Robotics has engaged over 1,000 teams across the United States, demonstrating its extensive reach and impact in STEM education.
